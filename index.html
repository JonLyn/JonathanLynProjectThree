Impuzzible

Created by jonCraftsCode at Juno College

A jigsaw puzzle game

How to play

  • Drag puzzle pieces to playing area and drop piece in the correct spot
  • Once all pieces are in the correct spot, you win!
  • You have 50 moves to complete the puzzle
  • Controls:
    • Click/Touch/Tab and hold on piece to drag
    • Release to drop - counts as 1 move
    • Tab key to select next piece
    • 'B' key to select previous piece
