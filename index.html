Impuzzible

Created by jonCraftsCode at Juno College

How to play

  • Drag puzzle pieces to playing area and drop piece in the correct spot
  • Once all pieces are in the correct spot, you win!
  • You have 50 moves total to complete the puzzle
  • Controls:
    • Click and hold mouse on piece to drag
    • Release mouse to drop
    • Tab or 'F' key to select next piece
    • 'B' key to select previous piece
